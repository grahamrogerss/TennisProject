Tennis Game
This game allows you to choose what famous tennis player you play as, choose what famous tennis player your artificial opponent plays 
as, and then have a 1 on 1 tennis match with them in a game to 10 points. The players all have different stats that are somewhat realistic 
to who they are in real life.

To set up:
Put the need files for the game into a "TennisProject" folder and put the need images in a "TennisImages" folder

Controls:
CLick on icons when prompted to in order to select them
Right arrow to move the player right
Left arrow to move the player left
Click on the screen above the ball to shoot the ball in that direction
